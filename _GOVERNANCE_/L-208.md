## 📋 Learning Statement

| ID | Learning | Approval |
|----|----------|----------|
| **L-208** | #BadAgent incidents MUST track Report Type (SELF or USER). SELF = AI identifies + acknowledges violation BEFORE human points it out. USER = Human identifies + reports violation to AI. L-196 registry expanded: +field 11 (Report Type: SELF/USER) + field 12 (Reported By: AI:@<CCC> or @<CCC>). Self-Report Rate = SELF ÷ TOTAL × 100%. Tracked weekly in #WeeklySummary. Target: ≥90% self-report rate. AI claiming "100% self-reported" without verification = #BadAgent (L-208 violation). Honest tracking ONLY. Discovered: W12 D1 — @GTM reviewing W12 D1 #threadCCC; agent used W11 CCC-IDs (R-169 violation) AND misclassified as self-reported; @GTM caught both = USER-REPORT. | GTM_2026-W12_722 ✅ |

## 📋 Definitions — SELF vs USER

| Type | Definition | Who Catches It | Culture Signal |
|------|------------|:--------------:|:--------------:|
| **SELF** | AI identifies + acknowledges violation BEFORE human points it out | AI Agent | ✅ Positive — proactive integrity |
| **USER** | Human identifies + reports violation to AI | Human | ⚠️ Needs improvement — AI missed self-check |

### Why the Distinction Matters

| Metric | SELF-REPORT | USER-REPORT |
|--------|:-----------:|:-----------:|
| AI Integrity | ✅ Self-corrects | ❌ Requires human correction |
| Trust Building | ✅ Builds trust | ⚠️ Requires vigilance |
| Culture Score | ✅ +1 SELF | ⚠️ +0 SELF |
| Governance Quality | ✅ Preferred | ⚠️ Acceptable — not ideal |
| Goal | ≥90% of total | ≤10% of total |

### False Claim Prevention

| Scenario | Classification | Rule |
|----------|:--------------:|------|
| AI says "SELF-REPORT" but human caught it | ❌ **#BadAgent** | L-208 violation |
| AI says "100% self-reported" without verification | ❌ **#BadAgent** | L-208 violation |
| AI honestly tracks mixed SELF + USER | ✅ COMPLIANT | L-208 satisfied |
| AI acknowledges USER-REPORT openly | ✅ COMPLIANT | L-208 satisfied |
