## 📋 Why #AgencyPRO #ProjectConnex

### The Problem

Financial advisors, CPAs, and service professionals need AI-powered tools to manage their practice — but enterprise AI platforms are too expensive ($50K+/yr) and consumer tools (ChatGPT) lack specialization.

### The Solution

**AgencyPRO** — a cooperatively-built AI platform for independent agency owners. Powered by connexOmni (multi-agent orchestration) and connexAthena (knowledge management). Sold as part of the BurnedOutAdvisor.com Retreat BUNDLE at $4,888 during #WeOwnSeason003 {Feb / Mar / Apr / May 2026}.

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **connexOmni** | Multi-agent AI orchestrator — task delegation, workflow automation |
| **connexAthena** | Knowledge management — RAG, document intelligence, context persistence |
| **AnythingLLM** | Platform — self-hosted, data sovereign, FOSS |
| **Cooperative** | Owned by ♾️ WeOwn.Agency 👥 — users benefit from platform improvements |

### Market Position

| Segment | Price | AgencyPRO Position |
|---------|-------|-------------------|
| Enterprise AI (Salesforce Einstein, etc.) | $50K-$500K/yr | ❌ Too expensive |
| Consumer AI (ChatGPT Pro) | $200/yr | ❌ Not specialized |
| **AgencyPRO** | **$4,888 (one-time BUNDLE)** | ✅ **Specialized + affordable + cooperative** |
